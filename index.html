About

"I am a Machine Learning Scientist at Expedia Group in London, currently working on problems in Search Engine Marketing (SEM). Other problems i have worked on include recommender systems, language models and learning-to-rank."

"Before joining Expedia Group, I spent 2 years in Vancouver as a PhD student in statistics at the university of British Columbia focusing on computational methods for Bayesian inference. Despite leaving the PhD early, i was able to publish at "ICML 2021 ". During my time in Vancouver I had the opportunity to work as a Research Scientist intern for Huawei Technologies focusing on machine learning fairness."

"I hold a MSc. in statistical sience from the university of Oxford and a BSc. in econometrics and operations research from the Erasmus university Rotterdam."
